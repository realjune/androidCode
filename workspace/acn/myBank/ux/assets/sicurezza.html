The safety of the conversation between the customer and the Bank is one of the main objectives in the development of online services.

Entering the on-line services you turn on encryption of the communication. All transactions with MY BANK ONLINE SERVICES occur on channel transmitting encrypted, the encryption algorithm used is SSL (Secure Socket Layer) with 256-bit digital key, which is recognized as the technology of encryption more secure line currently available.

LEncryption MY BANK ON-LINE SERVICES is certified by Thawte Certification Authority that ensures the veracity of the information transmitted is the identity of the order to block any attempts at phishing.

In this regard you have installed a 256-bit certificate with the following features:

  • certificate issued to the bank
  • made by: Thawte Server CA
  • renewed annually

This protection is activated automatically, without tasks to be performed by the customer, using the https protocol.

In order to further raise the level of security for the customer, for certain functions such as, for example, provision of credit, charging phone cards and prepaid cards, and others, is used the mechanism of One-Time Password by SMS to authorize the operation.

In addition, simultaneously with the execution of an operation dispositive will receive an SMS notification to mobile phones of the customer informing him of the type of activities performed.
